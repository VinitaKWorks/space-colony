Why Are We Here?

The Earth was becoming more and more unbearable for us to stay in. Our leaders have failed us, there were too many environmental disasters, and violence increased. For our safety, and future, we had to leave.

Light Source

In this city, we use a generator for all the high powered street lights to simulate Earths time periods throughout the day. These lights stay on for ten hours from 7:00 AM to 5:00 PM. After that, they switch to low power mode for four more hours until 9:00 PM. Then only a few street lights remain until 7:00 AM the next day. Light is very essential for humans to get by. Since we do not have enough light on the planet, it is very important that this generator is functioning well and that very few blackouts happen.

Agriculture

As of now, soil on this colony is not suitable for planting crops in. Therefore, we created a small farming field with soil entirely consisting of compost. The moment our city was built, it took the initiative to collect compost every week and place all of it in this field. Because compost is very essential to fostering the growth of plants, the town makes it a priority to collect as much as possible from citizens of the city. There are so many people who work in agriculture. Not only are there farmers, but there are also people who research and invent digital technologies for the purpose of farming.

Water Plant

The city has created a giant water plant. This plant is used to convert ice found on the moon into water. The plant extracts the ice from the craters of the moon, and then that ice goes through a quick melting process. After the ice melts, it gets delivered into peoples homes through pipes. There are also a few machines in the plant that collect water from the moons atmosphere and condense it into water suited for human consumption. All citizens are strongly encouraged to conserve water as it is rarer on the moon than on Earth.

Technology

This city contains a large campus of tech companies. The buildings on this campus consist of laboratories where new machines and devices are tested. As of now, the economy of our city (and entire planet) thrives on technology and tech workers. These workers plan and build machines which are used for generating water, energy, and food. They also innovate technologies from Earth such as phones and computers. This town contains only one satellite for WiFi connection, but this satellite is more powerful than the ones found on Earth.

Research Campus

On this campus, many adults in town come to this institution to learn about different subjects like math, technology, science, interterritorial relations, and more. This city contains some of the best researchers from planet Earth. Most of the time, these researchers are in charge of educating college students and other adults. However, they also occasionally convene very often to talk about how to accommodate more students and the future of education for the next generations.

The Bazaar

This is the downtown region of the city. Many people visit on a weekly (or sometimes daily) basis. This marketplace has many grocery stores, department stores, banks, and restaurants. Sometimes it gets a bit crowded, since it is one of the few places in the city for people to visit friends and peers.

City Hall

The city is very proud of its commitment to public service. The city hall is where a large group of people plan and execute very important decisions. Most policies proposed by the people here thus far involve approving technology and the citys infrastructure. Our public leaders have also been dedicated to upholding human rights for all. A key difference between this city and most Earths cities is that there is no single mayor, and that everyone in the city council is in charge of the execution of the towns laws.

Space Port

This is the station in which people travel from the colony to Earth and vice versa. People also come to the SpacePort to register for and confirm their citizenship in the city. The rocket near the spaceship is powered by mostly clean energy. It is highly capable of traveling long distances and it takes at most three days for a person to arrive at their destination.
